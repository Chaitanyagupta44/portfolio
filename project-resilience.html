Team-Based Resilience Intervention for Public Sector Staff

Designing sustainable wellbeing interventions for staff experiencing high stress and emotional labour

The Challenge

Staff survey results indicated high stress, anxiety, and unmet wellbeing needs among 100+ local office staff. Team Members faced prolonged demands, emotional labour, and limited organizational resources. A sustainable, ethical resilience intervention was needed.

My Approach

I designed a Team Leader-led intervention promoting evidence-based resilience behaviours through habit formation. Applied Job Demands-Resources Model, Psychological Capital framework, and PERMA. Re-contracted from one-off intervention to TL-led approach for scalability without sustained specialist involvement.

Impact

Created sustainable intervention materials that Team Leaders can deliver. Evaluated using RE-AIM framework. Designed for scalability across hundreds of offices if future evaluation confirms effectiveness. Client feedback described work as "outstanding".

Methods

Job Demands-Resources Model, Psychological Capital, PERMA, RE-AIM evaluation

Context

Setting: UK public sector (100+ staff)
Area: Wellbeing at Work
